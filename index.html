About Us

WE ARE THE LEADERS IN THE CONSTRUCTION INDUSTRY!

Our consultancy firm has been set up with a few energetic civil and environmental engineers, architects, scientists, town planners and retired horticulturists, following under the guidance of experienced retired engineers....
WHERE WE STAND…
Architecture and Structural design are more of an art, as much it is a science. To date, we have handled 300+ projects in various fields of civil engineering and architecture as one of the established consultants, we cater to the needs of the construction industry across the country. We have expertise in Architecture, Structural, Geo-Technical, Survey, and Project Management. We accept nothing but perfection that leads us to various states and other countries.
OUR APPROACH….
Quality and Excellence has always been our primary objective. Our consistent efforts have worked up to the complex requirements and expectations of the clients. Our dedicated team of architects and engineers give immense focus to planning and designing. Moreover, we provide consultancy for architecture, structural engineering, electrical engineering, and PH engineering, interior designing, heating, and ventilation systems, landscaping, fire-fighting and many more.

Services

OUR MISSION IS TO DELIVER THE BEST IN THE CATEGORY!

We are well versed in providing services such as designing framed structures for multi-storied buildings for residential complexes, shopping centres, hotels, and many more as well as interior decoration for the same. We aim to provide the customer... with one window service with approved plans, estimates, and design from development authorities. We conduct surveys of different lands, prepare contours maps and site layouts with our well-equipped labs approved by the Government of Odisha. We strengthen all types of damaged structure for further use. We provide necessary legal assistance for cases filed against industry and to assist in cess appeal cases. We also obtain permission for the release of minutes/forest land and licensing with the Government. We take care of recycling waste products as well as utilizing them as raw materials.
